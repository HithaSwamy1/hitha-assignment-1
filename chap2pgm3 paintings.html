Music Band

by Raja Ravi Vema

The portrait of Music Band was painted in 1806 by the Indian Classical artist Raja Ravi Verma, and today hangs in the Banglore. It is the third of three portraits of the Verma family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Gandhi Gallery,Banglore;
